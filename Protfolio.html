Inventory Management System

An inventory management system is a combination of technology and processes used to oversee and control the flow of goods through every stage of their lifecycle, from raw materials to finished products ready for sale. It helps businesses maintain the right amount of inventory, ensuring products are available when needed without excess stock that ties up capital.

Dark web Monitoring system

A dark web monitoring system is a cybersecurity tool that continuously scans the hidden, encrypted parts of the internet—known as the dark web—for stolen or compromised data related to an organization or individual. It searches for sensitive information like passwords, personal data, or corporate details that may have been leaked or traded on illicit forums and marketplaces.
